splonks-rs leaf-first port order
================================

Purpose:
- classify modules by dependency weight before porting
- keep the literal port focused on real leaves first
- avoid forcing fake scaffolding into non-leaf modules too early

Note on `mod.rs` files
----------------------
- yes, they are kind of cheating
- most of them are just module wiring, not real behavioral code
- track them separately so they do not distract from the actual leaf pass
